## Usage Guidelines

1. **Mix and match** templates with power words for variety
2. **Don't overuse** power words - 1-2 per title is ideal
3. **Keep titles under 60 characters** for SEO
4. **Include the year** (2026) for freshness signals
5. **Use location names** for local SEO targeting
6. **Test different templates** to see what performs best
